# My Ideal Customer Profile

Customize this file and save it as `icp.md` in your project root.
The lead qualifier skill reads this file automatically.

## Hard criteria (disqualify if not met)

- Must be a B2B company (not consumer)
- Must be in software, tech-enabled services, or professional services
- Must have at least 20 employees

## Soft criteria (score but don't automatically disqualify)

- Company size: 50-500 employees (ideal: 100-250)
- Geography: North America or Western Europe
- Stage: Series A through Series C, or profitable SMB
- Annual revenue: $2M - $50M ARR

## Strong positive signals (boost score)

- Recently raised funding (last 18 months)
- Actively hiring sales or revenue roles
- Using tools that indicate scaling pain (Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach)
- Fast headcount growth (>30% YoY on LinkedIn)
- Founder-led sales transitioning to a sales team

## Negative signals (reduce score but don't disqualify)

- No dedicated sales team
- Very early stage (pre-seed, no product yet)
- Highly regulated industry (may extend sales cycle)
